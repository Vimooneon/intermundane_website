                  HYDRO
The water cat

 About:
Hydro was another experiment of the lab, using a refined version of the liquid previously used to revive other experimental subjects as well as in combination with equipment for advanced water control he was made from a cat skeleton. The test subject behaved themselves calmly and was obedient to given commands, so the experiment was deemed successful. It also seemed Hydro has no recollection of his past life before revival which eased his transition to working for the lab. Hydro is often seen unemotional with hollow expression.

 Powers:
Hydro was equipped with outer worldly technology to be able to control water. He can link to nearby water sources, allowing to drain water from them. The water flows out from indentation on the neck and wraps around like a collar. Lab mainly used him as portable water dispenser so most of his work was within the building rather than venturing outside it. It is also known that if the water source he links to gets contaminated he will also get contaminated, so the water source must remain clean at all times.
