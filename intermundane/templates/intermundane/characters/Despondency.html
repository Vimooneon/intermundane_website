                  DESPONDENCY
The vampiric wolf

 About:
Despondency was born in a world with a layer of forest so wide almost no sunlight ever comes to the ground, with dangerous creatures unkown to our world roaming around. Having never known family love nor meanings of friendships Despondency clearly knew only one thing - in this world it's kill or be killed. From a young age Despondency had to push his sense to the limit, it was the only means of survival in that environment.

 Powers:
One day he was deadly wounded by a creature he fought. Yet, to his surprise he was saved by glowing butterflies who seemed to had some sort of vampiric ability. The could make others inherit their own bloodthirst and that led Despondency to obtain new - blood related powers. Upon his "rebirth" not only his senses sharpened even more than from years of training in the forest, a third eye appeared on his forehead, not to see but to sense blood around him instead, didn't matter if creature was behind him, hiding, or completely out of sight, Despondency could know exactly where it was at any point of time.
