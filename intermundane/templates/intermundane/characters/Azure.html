                  AZURE
The angelic cat
 About:
Known as the guiding light that will help others find their path, Azure existed for milenia of years and been worshipped by some civilizations of the past. Although Azure is rarely ever seen outside of his domain, statues and scriptures telling legends of his presence can be found in abandoned cities of the past and ancient temples.

 Powers:
Using the power of the golden eye on his chest, Azure can observe any places Azure knows about, regardless of distance and obstacles in the way. Whenever Azure uses this power, which is most of the time, the golden eye on his chest is open. The vision of the eye does not interfere with Azures normal vision so he can view places through golden eye at the same time as seeing what's infront of him.
