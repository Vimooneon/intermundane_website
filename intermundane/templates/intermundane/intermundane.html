About Intermundane

A laboratory focused on investigating alternate dimentions finally discovers a way to travel between worlds. However, the possible dangers contained inside these worlds are unkown, thus starting a program to start mapping and documenting these without risks...for them.
The story follows Irarenai, a test subject brought back form the dead for a singular task: explore and document as much as physically possible and report back to the lab with the information.

Lore significant locations

The Lab

The place where everything began and the anchor of the story, while the ethics of some of the experiments running here can be questioned, the technological advancement is undeniable

The Desert

A world that resembles the Egypt known in our planet, except with capability of reaching much higher temperatures and containing unknown technologies inside the mysterious pyramids

The Forest

The ever-dark forest is among the most dangerous worlds in the lab's records, not only due to a lack of any natural lighting, but also the dangerous inhabitants of this place
